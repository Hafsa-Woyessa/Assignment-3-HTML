Early Life

Mark Zuckerberg was born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York, into a comfortable, well-educated family. His father worked at a dental practice ,while his mother worked as a psychiatrist. He developed an interest in computers at a young age and created various messaging programs for fun to communicate with his friends and family.

He attended Phillips Exeter Academy in New Hampshire, where he won prizes in science, math, astronomy, physics and classical studies. In 2002 he attended Harvard University where he and his fellow roommates Dustin Moskovitz, Chris Hughes and Eduardo Saverin founded thefacebook.com known today as Facebook. In his sophomore year , Mark dropped out of college to focus on developing Facebook, moving to Palo Alto, California.

On February 4, 2004, at 19 years old Marck and his fellow friends launched "Thefacebook", later changed to Facebook the following year had more than 1 million registered users and the newly-founded company had not only moved to Silicon Valley but also secured a $500,000 investment from PayPal co-founder Peter Thiel.

That same year he was added to MIT Technology Review's TR35 list as one of the top 35 innovators in the world under the age of 35.

In 2007, at age 23, he became the world's youngest self-made billionaire.

On May 18, 2012 Facebook held its first initial public offering (IPO). It was the biggest in technology and one of the biggest in Internet history, with a peak market capitalization of over $104 billion.

The next day Mark Zuckerberg and his current wife Prisicllia Chan got married.

In 2013, Facebook made the Fortune 500 list for the first time, making Mark Zuckerberg, at the age of 28, the youngest CEO on the list.

In December 2015, Mark Zuckerebeg and his wife, Priscilla Chan, pledged to give away 99% of their Facebook stake over their lifetimes to different charity organizations such as.

As of May 2021, Zuckerberg's net worth is $117.8 billion, making him the 5th-richest person in the world.

Contributions

His most famous and recent contributions in the computer science field was his creation of the e social-networking website Facebook . Facebook, a social networking service that had only 1 million users at the end of 2004 but now has about 1.96 billion users worldwide. The app has not only raised the bar for fellow computer programmers but has revolutionized modern technology and communication in the 21st century.

Mark Zuckerberg created the largest social media platform that helped pave the way for non-physical interactions, spread of information,and addressing social and political issues globally.

On May 24, 2007, Zuckerberg created Facebook Platform, a development platform for programmers to create social applications within Facebook. It grew to more than 800,000 developers around the world building applications for Facebook Platform.

On August 20, 2013 he launched Internet.org ,later renamed the Free Basics, an app that would improve internet access and help to provide it to people in developing countries.

Since 2008, Time magazine has named Zuckerberg among the 100 most influential people in the world as a part of its Person of the Year award. In December 2016, Zuckerberg was ranked 10th on Forbes list of The World's Most Powerful People.
